FAQS

Easy to cancel booking. To cancel a booking, go to the website or app where you made the reservation, find your booking in your account or by using a confirmation number, and follow the prompts to cancel. The cancellation process and refund eligibility depend on the service (e.g., airline, hotel, train) and the specific terms and conditions of your booking.

Quick and easy Booking. To book a travel destination, you can use an online booking platform to search for and reserve flights, hotels, or complete tour packages. Popular destinations include both domestic and international locations like Dubai, Bangkok, Kerala, and Goa. When booking, consider factors like the type of trip you want, your budget, and when you want to travel.

Login.tto log in, find the "Sign in" button, enter your username or email and password, and click "Log in". If prompted, you may need to enter a verification code. For a computer login, you may need to enter your password or PIN on the sign-in screen.

UPI/Card PaymentTo make an online payment on a website, you can use a digital wallet like Google Pay or Apple Pay, enter your credit/debit card details directly, or use a third-party service like PayPal. The process generally involves adding your chosen payment method to a secure checkout page, which then sends your information through a payment gateway to be authorized by your bank or card issuer.

Under 18Charges for a person under 18 in India are governed by the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection) Act, 2015, which categorizes offenses as petty, serious, or heinous. For "heinous" crimes committed by a minor between 16 and 18, a Juvenile Justice Board will assess if they should be tried as an adult. For other offenses, minors face different processes and potential punishments compared to adults.

Refundto get a refund from a bank, contact customer service or visit a branch to formally request the refund, providing all details like your account number, transaction date, and amount. If you are seeking a refund for an unauthorized transaction, you will need to provide proof of the incorrect debit and, if applicable, evidence of fraud. Be prepared to fill out a dispute form, either online or in person, and have your ID and other necessary documents ready.
