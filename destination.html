GOLDEN TEMPLE

The Golden Temple[a] is a gurdwara located in Amritsar, Punjab, India.[3][4] It is the pre-eminent spiritual site of Sikhism. It is one of the holiest sites in Sikhism, alongside the Gurdwara Darbar Sahib Kartarpur in Kartarpur, and Gurdwara Janam Asthan in Nankana Sahib, both in Punjab, Pakistan.

LUCKNOW

Lucknow (Hindi: Lakhanaū, pronounced [ˈləkʰnə.uː] ⓘ) is a metropolis and the second largest city of the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh where it serves as the capital and the administrative headquarters of the eponymous district and division. The city had a population of 2.8 million.
